Reading the family map

  1. Move around: pan and zoom the map when the family gets wide.
  2. Find person: type a name to jump straight to one relative.
  3. Selected person: click a card to see birthday, parents, partner, children, and profile links in this sidebar.
  4. Lines: green paths connect partners, parents, and children across generations.
  5. C1, C2: couple markers show which parent group a child branch belongs to.

Tip: open a profile when you want the full story or need to edit details.
